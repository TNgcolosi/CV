I am a lamp stack web development student at CodeSpace in Cape Town. My coding journey started earlier this year when I was working on a CSI project with high school students and realised that they needed more exposure to tech resources and knowledge. I found out about Code4CT, sent a student to learn there, and was inspired by my student to learn to program myself. I am fascinated by what I have discovered so far and am eager to solve real world problems with my new tech skills, especially problems specific to the socio-economic dynamics of South Africa. Have a look at my code community to see more of my work that brings young people in contact with technology.
